Rogue Rover

Can you help NASA find their lost rover on Mars?

Instructions

  • A Mars rover has gone rogue and is travelling randomly around a plateau on Mars. You need to navigate the remaining rover to the same grid reference as the rogue rover to dock with it. Only then can the NASA engineers regain control of it.
  • Click the Start Game button and choose your starting grid square.
  • Each turn you must enter up to 10 instructions for your rover to follow.
  • The only instructions allowed are:
    • R - Turn 90 degrees right (clockwise)
    • L - Turn 90 degrees left (anticlockwise)
    • M - Move one space forwards.
    • Example: LMLMLMLM will send your rover in a small circuit ending up in exactly the same place it started.
  • On your display you can see your current coordinates and heading
